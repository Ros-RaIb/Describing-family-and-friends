📚 Grammar

Types of Adjectives

Selecciona cada tipo para aprender más / Click to explore each type

Descriptive Adjectives
Describe qualities or physical characteristics of people — Describen cualidades o características físicas.
tallshortslimstrongyoungoldintelligentfunnykindshy
Examples
She is tall and slim.
My brother is very funny.
He has long, blonde hair.
Possessive Adjectives
Show who something or someone belongs to — Indican posesión o pertenencia.
myyourhisheritsourtheir
Structure: Possessive + Noun
My sister is very kind.
Her mother is a teacher.
Their grandfather is wise.
Opinion Adjectives
Express a personal judgment or point of view — Expresan un juicio personal.
lovelyamazingwonderfulinterestinggreatterribleawful
Examples
My dad is an amazing cook.
She is a wonderful person.
He is an interesting guy.

🗣️ Speaking Time

Your turn to Speak!

Actividad oral — ¡Habla en voz alta!

📋
Instructions: Think of a family member or close friend. Use the prompts below to describe them in 4–5 sentences. Then share with a classmate!
Step 1 · Who?
Who are you describing?
"I'm going to describe my ___."

e.g. "I'm going to describe my best friend, Carlos."
Step 2 · Appearance
What do they look like?
"He / She is ___ and has ___."

e.g. "She is tall and slim. She has long, black hair."
Step 3 · Personality
What is their personality like?
"He / She is very ___."

e.g. "He is very funny, loyal and creative."
Step 4 · Relationship
What is your relationship like?
"I get on well with ___ because ___."

e.g. "I get on well with her because she is honest."
📝 Full Example
"I'm going to describe my mother. She is short and slim. She has long, dark hair and brown eyes. She is very caring and patient. I get on well with her because she is always supportive."
